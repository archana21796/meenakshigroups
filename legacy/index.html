Our Journey

For more than seven decades, the Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions has been shaping the future of education in Chennai. Managed by the IIET Society, the group has consistently focused on academic excellence, innovation and holistic development.

With a legacy of over 75 years, the group has established four premier institutions that nurture thousands of students across engineering, management, arts and school education.
