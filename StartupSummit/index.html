OUR MISSION

The Mission

Innovators Fest is the flagship startup event of the MS Innovation & Incubation Centre. We provide a high-velocity environment where founders can bridge the gap between concept and commercialization through mentorship, funding, and a thriving innovation community.

Register Your Idea

Submit your idea and take the first step toward transforming a concept into a real venture.

Get Incubated

Selected ideas will receive mentorship, guidance, and access to a structured incubation pathway.

Build What Matters

Develop impactful solutions with support from industry mentors, innovators, and investors.

Startup Journey Roadmap

From idea submission to final presentation.

Pre-Registration Opens

Pitch Deck Submission

Submit your startup pitch deck outlining the problem, solution, market opportunity, and business model.

Before 20 March

Evaluation Phase

Industry mentors and experts evaluate submitted ideas based on feasibility, innovation, and scalability.
