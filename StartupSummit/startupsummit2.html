Judging Framework

Evaluation Criteria

We are not looking for flashy slides. We are looking for founders in the making.

01
Problem Clarity
How clearly and deeply the problem is defined, validated, and understood with supporting evidence.
02
Innovation Quotient
The uniqueness, originality, and creative leap of the proposed solution in the market.
03
Feasibility
Practical viability of building and implementing the idea with available or acquirable resources.
04
Impact Potential
The potential to create meaningful, real-world value and disrupt an existing market or behavior.
05
Team Strength
The capability, clarity, commitment, and complementary skills of the founding team behind the idea.
