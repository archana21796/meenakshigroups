About the Event

Where Dynamic Minds Converge

MSSM VIBHA 26 is the flagship annual inter-collegiate management festival of Meenakshi Sundararajan School of Management, celebrating a proud 25 years of excellence. VIBHA stands for Visionary Intelligence for Business Excellence.

From creative ad blitzes to stock market simulations, from corporate runway walks to Shark Tank-style pitches — VIBHA 26 offers a thrilling mix of events that test every dimension of business talent. Whether UG or PG, your stage awaits.
