About VIBHA

VIBHA is a premier inter-college management fest organized by the Meenakshi Sundararajan School of Management (MSSM). Designed to foster innovation, creativity, and strategic thinking, VIBHA 2026 brings together the brightest minds from across the region to compete in a series of high-stakes business challenges and creative showcases.
