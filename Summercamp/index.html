Frequently Asked Questions

What is the age limit for the summer camp?

The MSGI Summer Camp is specially designed for children between the ages of 15 and 17 years.

Where is the summer camp conducted?

The camp is held at the Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions (MSGI) campus located in Kodambakkam, Chennai.

What activities are included in the camp?

We offer a wide range of activities including Robotics, AI, IoT, Arts, Martial Arts, Pickleball, Soccer, and Theatre.

How do I register for the camp?

You can register directly on this website by filling out the form at the top of the page. Once submitted, our counselor will call you.
