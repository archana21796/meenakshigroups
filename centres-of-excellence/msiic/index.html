Our Vision & Mission

The Meenakshi Sundararajan Innovation Incubation Center (MSIIC) is established with the vision to foster an environment where young minds can transform innovative ideas into reality, driving entrepreneurship and technological advancement. MSIIC aims to be a catalyst for creating sustainable business solutions and fostering collaborations between academia, industry, and entrepreneurial ecosystems.
