About MSGI

Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions

With over 75 years of educational heritage, the Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions (MSGI) is one of Tamil Nadu's most trusted academic groups. Based in Chennai, MSGI comprises four institutions, each recognised for academic rigour, industry integration, and exceptional placement outcomes.
