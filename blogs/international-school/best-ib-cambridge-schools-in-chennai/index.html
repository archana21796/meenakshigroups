Our Philosophy

Why IB & Cambridge Curriculum?

At The Nest School, we firmly believe that rote memorization is not education. Cramming facts to pass tests does not prepare children for the challenges — or opportunities — of the modern world.

That's why we chose the IB and Cambridge curricula: frameworks that dismantle the outdated education model and replace it with something far more powerful — inquiry-based learning.

🔍 Inquiry-Based 🧠 Critical Thinking 🌍 Globally Recognized 🛠 Real-World Skills 🎨 Creative & Confident

Our students don't just learn answers — they learn how to ask better questions. They explore ideas, challenge assumptions, connect disciplines, and develop the intellectual independence to thrive on any global stage.
