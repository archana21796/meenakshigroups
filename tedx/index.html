TEDxTNS Youth – TEDx Event in Chennai

TEDxTNS Youth is a TEDx event hosted at The NEST School, part of the Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions,
bringing together young innovators and powerful TEDx Talks in Chennai.

What is
TEDx ?

TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group.

TEDxTNS Youth is an independently organized TED event hosted by The NEST School, Kodambakkam, Chennai - 60. The event brings together bright young minds and changemakers to share powerful stories that challenge perspectives and ignite local action.
