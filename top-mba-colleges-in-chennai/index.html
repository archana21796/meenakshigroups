About MSSM

A Quarter Century of Management Excellence

Meenakshi Sundararajan School of Management has been shaping the next generation of business leaders in Chennai for over 25 years. As one of the best MBA colleges in Chennai, MSSM combines the academic rigour of a University of Madras affiliated institution with a deeply practical, industry-connected approach to management education.

Our journey is one of consistent academic achievement, meaningful industry partnerships, and a genuine commitment to student success. MSSM graduates carry with them not just an MBA qualification, but the professional networks, management competencies, and ethical foundations needed to lead in today's dynamic business landscape.

With a robust alumni network of over 1000 professionals working across finance, marketing, human resources, and general management, MSSM offers students an unparalleled ecosystem of mentorship, placement support, and career growth from the moment they join.
